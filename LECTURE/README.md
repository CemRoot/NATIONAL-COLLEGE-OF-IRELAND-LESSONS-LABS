## ğŸ“ Study Tips

### Making the Most of Lectures

1. **Attend Actively** - Engage with the material during class
2. **Take Notes** - Write summaries in your own words
3. **Ask Questions** - Clarify concepts during or after class
4. **Review Regularly** - Don't wait until exams
5. **Connect to Labs** - Apply lecture concepts in practical exercises

### Effective Note-Taking

- Use structured formats (Cornell notes, mind maps)
- Highlight key concepts and definitions
- Include examples and applications
- Note areas for further study
- Cross-reference with textbooks
